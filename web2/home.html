#IAmGTComputing
A fun project that I did for work at the Georgia Tech College of Computing. This was a super fun shoot. Shot on a Canon Rebel T3i DSLR.
Mofilm Coke Commercial
The first assignment of my Video Production class. We were given the summer 2014 prompt for our first assignemnt, which was as follows: Make a commercial for Cocacola that appeals to either teenagers or single mothers. We chose to go with the latter group of people. Shot on two Canon Rebel T3i cameras.
Comfort
My final project for my Video Production class. A short film about a girl who gets abducted by a water spirit. Shot on two Canon Rebel T3i DSLR cameras.
[in]Dependent
The second project for my Documentary Film class. We chose to compare and contrast a small bookstore in East Atlanta Villiage with a larger bookstore chain. Shot on a Canon HD Camcorder
